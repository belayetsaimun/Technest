Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our deals

How long do the flash sales last?

Our flash sales typically last between 24-72 hours, depending on the promotion. Each deal has its own countdown timer showing exactly when the offer expires. Once the timer reaches zero, the deal will automatically end and prices will return to normal.

Can I combine multiple discounts or coupons?

Most of our promotions cannot be combined with other offers. Each product can only have one discount applied at checkout. However, free shipping coupons can often be used alongside product discounts. For specific information about combining offers, please check the terms and conditions of each coupon or contact our customer support team.

What happens if a deal sells out before the timer ends?

All our deals are available while supplies last. Once a product sells out, the deal will be marked as "Sold Out" even if the timer hasn't expired. We recommend adding items to your cart as soon as possible to secure your discount, as popular deals tend to sell out quickly.

Are sale items eligible for returns?

Yes, all sale items follow our standard return policy of 30 days, unless otherwise specified in the product description. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund or exchange within 30 days of delivery. Please note that the item must be in its original condition with all packaging and accessories.

How do I know if a deal is good value?

We always display the original price alongside the sale price, so you can see exactly how much you're saving. The percentage discount and total amount saved are also clearly shown. Our original prices are genuine and were in effect for at least 30 days before being discounted, giving you confidence that you're getting a real deal.
